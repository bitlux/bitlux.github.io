Construction Central

Accessible Memberships

Construction Central is one of the largest and most diverse maker spaces in the San Francisco Bay Area. It facilitates many maker disciplines such as woodworking, metal fabrication, textiles, 3D printing, laser cutting, and electronics. It offers a wide variety of classes and workshops. Construction Central members can design and create anything they can dream of using software and machinery, connect with a vibrant community, and learn new ways to build almost anything!

While Construction Central is funded primarily though paid monthly memberships, it also believes that financial hardship should not be a barrier to being a part of the space. CC's mission is to provide access to our facility to the community, so it are proud to offer a limited number of no-cost, 6-month Accessible Memberships to those who can benefit.

Benefits

Recipients of an Accessible Memberships will have access to Construction Central for a 6-month membership. They will have the same rights, privileges, and responsiblities as a paid member. They will also receive a CC gift card which can be used to pay for classes, purchase materials, and/or rent storage spaces.

Expectations

The same rules that apply to paid members also apply to Accessible Membership recipients. Specifically:

  • Training classes are required to use certain machines safely and independently.
  • Certain areas require personal protective equipment (PPE). Some communal PPE is available; however everyone is encouraged to bring their own.
  • Some scrap material is available; members are encouraged to obtain sufficient stock as necessary.
  • Members need to provide your own transportation. Construction Central is biking distance from a light rail station.

Accessible Memberships are for adults (18+) only.

Application process

To apply for an Accessible Membership, please fill out this form. A committee will review applications and select prospective members to follow up with. Prospective members will be expected to visit Construction Central for an on-site tour of the facility 1-2 weeks after the application due date. Prospective members will also be expected to provide evidence of financial need. Accepted members will be notified two weeks before the start of the membership.

Current and former Accessible Membership recipients may be eligible to re-apply for the next cohort.
